# PRODUCT.md — Angola360

## Product Purpose
Angola360 is an interactive tourist portal for Angola. It is a full-screen map application: cartographic first, editorial second. Users navigate Angola's 20 real points of interest through 2D/3D maps and 360° immersive views, guided by an AI assistant (Claude).

## Register
product

## Users
Travel-minded users discovering Angola (domestic and international), researchers, and adventure seekers. Desktop/laptop use, normal office lighting or dim evening. They want to explore, not book — discovery-focused.

## Brand
- **Name**: Angola360
- **Mark**: Stylized water drop / teardrop with concentric ring — map pin meets raindrop
- **Tone**: Knowledgeable guide. Evocative and proud of Angola. Not touristy or promotional.
- **Anti-references**: Generic travel agency sites. Safari stock-photo galleries. Neon tourism posters. SaaS dashboards. Booking.com UI.
- **Language**: Portuguese (European)

## Design Principles
1. The map is the product. UI chrome must recede and support, not compete.
2. Gold (#C9A84C) is earned — active states, AI moments, brand marks only. Not decoration.
3. Dark mode is intentional: an explorer studying maps at night, not a hotel booking page.
4. Every text element should feel authored. No AI-generated boilerplate vibes.
